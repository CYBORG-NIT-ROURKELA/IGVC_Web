IGVC

About

The Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition is an annual international robotics competition for teams of undergraduate and graduate students. Teams design and build an autonomous ground vehicle capable of completing several difficult challenges.

Cyborg

About

The Official Robotics and Automation Club of National Institute of Technology,Rourkela registered under Student Activity Cantre (SAC). Founded in 2008, it continues to nurture brilliant minds to work dilligently to shape itself better every year.

Our Subsystems





Software Subsystem

We design intelligence.



The main aim of the software subsystem is to develop modular and error-free software, to get the vehicles working for challenging tasks.

Electronics Subsystem

We build connections.



The main aim of the electronics subsystem is to make the bridge between the software stack of the vehicle and the outside world.

Mechanical Subsystem

We build from scratch.



The goal of the mechanical subsystem is to sketch a modular and portable design in Solidworks and deliver full potential to all the components of the vehicle.

Web Development

We write codes.



Good tools make website development quicker and easier to maintain than if you did everything by hand.
